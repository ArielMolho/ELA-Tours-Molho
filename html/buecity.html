Buenos Aires like a local

Set off on a 5-hour walking tour with our local expert guide, learning all about the history and culture of "Mi Buenos Aires querido" - My beloved Buenos Aires. Our local expert guide is 100% "Puro Porteño" (resident of the city), who knows each barrio like the back of their hand. Together, you'll head off to explore some of city's historical hotspots, as well as some of the favourite local hangouts with your guide. It's a great mix of experiences thrown into one. Getting an inside glimpse of Buenos Aires, you'll go far beyond the guidebooks to avoid the touristy crowds, allowing you to see a different side of the city. At times, you'll take public transport to cover the larger distances, while most of the tour will be on foot.
After a few hours with your personal guide, the tour will finish at one of their favourite restaurants, to grab a well-deserved bite to eat if you so desire (lunch not included). This experience is probably one of the most insightful ways to have a peek into the real porteño way of life.

Notes: Tour Lenght is 5 hours. Wear comfortable footwear. Departures are daily at time to be determined. The tour doesn´t operate on January 1st, May 1st, December 24th, December 25th & December 31st.
